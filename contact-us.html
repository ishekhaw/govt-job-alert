Frequently Asked Questions

How often are job alerts updated?

Our website is updated multiple times daily with the latest government job notifications from official sources. Check back regularly for the most current openings.

Are you affiliated with official government websites?

No, Government Job Alert is an independent job alert aggregator. We collect and display job notifications from official government sources for your convenience. Please visit official websites for verification.

How can I report a missing or incorrect job posting?

If you notice any missing or incorrect information, please email us at support@govtjobalert.net with details about the issue.

Do you charge any fees?

Government Job Alert is completely free to use. We do not charge any fees for accessing job notifications or resources on our website.

Can I get notifications sent to my email?

We're working on an email subscription feature. Please check back soon or contact us to express your interest.

How do I apply for a job?

Each job listing links to the official website or PDF. Follow the instructions provided by the official recruitment agency. Government Job Alert does not process applications directly.
